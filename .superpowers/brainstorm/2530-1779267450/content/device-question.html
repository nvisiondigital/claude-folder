Welk apparaat gebruikt Florian op locatie?

This shapes the entire design — a phone-first app looks very different from a laptop tool

A

📱 Smartphone

Florian uses his phone on-site to take photos and fill in the form. App needs to be very easy to use with one hand, large buttons, camera integration.

B

📟 Tablet

Florian carries a tablet on-site. More screen space, easier form filling, still takes photos with it.

C

💻 Laptop on-site + phone for photos

Florian takes photos with his phone and fills in the form on his laptop — either on-site or back at the office.

D

🖥️ Laptop at the office only

Photos are transferred after the visit. Florian fills everything in at his desk. Desktop-first design.
